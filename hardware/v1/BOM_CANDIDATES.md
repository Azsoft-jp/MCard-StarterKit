# V1 BOM candidates

This shortlist converts the V1 architecture into parts that can be evaluated in
KiCad. It is not an approved production BOM. JLCPCB/LCSC stock, price,
basic/extended classification, lifecycle, and assembly eligibility are
time-sensitive and must be checked again at schematic freeze and immediately
before ordering.

The target is an 8.5 mm-class ESP32-S3 BLE badge with a 2.0 inch-class SPI TFT,
dynamic NFC tag, LiPo power, USB-C, side lighting, three buttons, piezo sound,
and an optional vibration footprint. V1 does not include an NFC reader IC,
metal enclosure assumptions, real-device OTA flashing, or a 6.7 mm finished
thickness claim.

## Decision Pending

- Exact ESP32-S3 module package: WROOM-1-N16R8 is preferred, but the current
  floorplan must prove its antenna keepout and 3.1 mm height fit. MINI-1-N4R2
  is the compact fallback; MINI-1-N8R8 is not an approved assumption.
- Exact display FPC pinout: no schematic release until the ordered panel
  drawing and sample agree on pin order, contact side, backlight, and tail datum.
- Exact NFC tag part: ST25DV04KC-IE6S3 is preferred; NTAG I2C plus requires a
  separate symbol, footprint, antenna review, and firmware driver.
- Battery connector style: JST SH is the preferred detachable option; soldered
  leads may be thinner but require strain-relief and serviceability review.
- Regulator topology: TPS63031 buck-boost is the current direction, pending a
  measured power budget and load-transient result.
- Vibration motor inclusion: DNP by default until stack height, stall current,
  vibration, and EMI tests pass.
- RGB LED package: SK6805-EC15 is a sourcing candidate, not frozen until its
  supply/data thresholds and optical coupling are verified.
- PCB stackup/thickness: 0.8 mm four-layer remains preferred and 1.0 mm is the
  fallback; use current JLCPCB quote options and process rules at layout freeze.

- No part in this file is an authorization to order.

  Verify the exact module or finished-product certification from an official
