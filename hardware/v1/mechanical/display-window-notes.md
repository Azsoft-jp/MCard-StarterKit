# V1 display window notes

Status: display-window planning, not a released drawing.

## Baseline

- Use a 2.0 inch-class 240 x 320 portrait IPS TFT.
- Keep the window in the upper flat-front zone.
- Keep the display stack inside the 6.8 mm LCD/body region.
- Keep the display keepout separated from the lower rear LiPo keepout.

## Window and retention rules

- Reference the clear window to the active area and visible area from the selected display drawing.
- Keep adhesive land, dust mask, light mask, ribs, and retention ledges outside the active area.
- Confirm the panel tail and connector can be assembled without crossing the BLE keepout, rear thickness transition, USB-C opening, or battery keepout.
- Avoid front steps or raised bezels that break the flat front datum.
- Use transparent smoke resin optical review for haze, tint, internal appearance, and RGB light leakage.
