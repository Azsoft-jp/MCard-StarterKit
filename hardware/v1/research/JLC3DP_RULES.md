## V1 application

- Use a nonconductive resin rear case and prototype fit before cosmetic finish.
- Choose material/process before setting minimum wall, ribs, bosses, snap fits,
  hole allowance, engraved text, and unsupported features.
- Design drainage/cleaning access and protect display, battery, and antennas
  from assembly stress.
- Export a watertight solid in the unit/orientation expected by the service and
  inspect the online model.
