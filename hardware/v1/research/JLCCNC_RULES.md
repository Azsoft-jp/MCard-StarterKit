## Planning rules

- Keep aluminum back-cover work in V2/V3 until BLE/NFC antenna effects are
  simulated, prototyped, measured, and reviewed.
- Future handoff must define material, temper, finish, critical tolerances,
  datums, threads, corner radii, tool access, cosmetic faces, and no-metal RF
  volumes.
- Do not transfer resin allowances directly to CNC.
